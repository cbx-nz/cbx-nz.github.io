MDMA Awareness

What is MDMA?

MDMA, also known as Ecstasy or Molly, is a psychoactive drug that affects mood and perception. It is commonly used at parties and music festivals.

Risks and Dangers

MDMA can cause dehydration, overheating, and increased heart rate. Long-term use may lead to memory issues, anxiety, and depression.

Harm Reduction Tips

If you or someone you know chooses to use MDMA, stay hydrated, avoid mixing substances, and test substances when possible.
